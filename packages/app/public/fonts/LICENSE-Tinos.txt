Tinos (Regular) — the HUD's vector text face.

Tinos is a metric-compatible substitute for a classic transitional serif ("Times"-class),
used here as a near-identical, sharp vector stand-in for the original game's UI bitmap font
(see packages/app/src/content/ui-font.ts for why). These files are the Latin and
Latin-Extended (Polish diacritics) woff2 subsets published by Fontsource / Google Fonts.
