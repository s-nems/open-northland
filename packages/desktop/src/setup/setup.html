Open Northland

Open Northland is a free re-implementation of Cultures — 8th Wonder of the World. It ships no game art or sounds: on this first run it converts the assets of your own copy of the original game into its data folder. Select the folder your game is installed in (the one with Game.exe and DataX) to begin.

The free community mod CulturesNation is required to play, and your game folder does not have it. Open Northland can download it for you (~600 MB, from culturesnation.pl's Google Drive link) into its data folder — your game folder stays untouched.
